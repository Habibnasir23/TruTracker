One is able to select their current location and destination on the home screen via the dropdown menus present on the home page. From there, one may select the corresponding door they wish to use to enter and exit.

TruTracker is built with the help of publicly available external mapping software. As such, buildings may be misnamed, not up to date, or edited. Differing building names do not reflect the TruTracker team's beliefs or opinions.

While not currently available, a text version of directions is in the works from the TruTracker team.

The TruTracker program is built to only include recognized trails, roads, and pathways. Many common routes across the Truman campus utilize so-called “Desire Paths”, which the TruTracker team does not recognize as legitimate pathways due to safety and liability concerns.

In order to make use of TruTracker’s custom locations functionality, one must make and utilize an account.
